About Me

When I was in third grade, I was visiting my grandmother, who happened to be making granny squares. I asked her what she was doing. Rather than giving any verbal explanation, she found another crochet hook and another skein of yarn, and handed them to me. Then she proceded to show me how to make granny squares. From that time on I was "hooked."

The next year my mother began teaching me how to knit. Since then she has knitted sweaters that have won ribbons at the county fair. I couldn't ask for a better instructor.

When I was in high school, I had the opportunity to take a class in Fortran, which is now an archaic programming language. Back then it was state of the art, and I gladly accepted the challenge. I took to computer programming like a duck to water, and found another passion. I eventually obtained a B.S. degree in computer science and worked with computers in a variety of capacities.

Now I am combining these passions, using my computer skills to create a website about knitting and crocheting. I recently decided to expand my computer skill set, and signed up for a website design class held by BitWise Industries . As a student of BitWise, I am continuing to learn new techniques and will be implementing some of them in this website. The previous version of Creative Yarn Crafts may be viewed for comparison.
